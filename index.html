Hi, I'm a technical writer who loves clear & concise writing.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Lake Forest Park, Washington, with my fiancé and our dog and three cats. My journey into technical writing started with working closely with technical writers as an executive assistant at a software company. I was fascinated with crafting content that was usable for its intended audience. I was hooked and wanted to transition to a career in technical writing. I supplemented my already strong background in writng, and my bachelor's degree in Economics, with a certificate in technical writing.

Now, I'm a Technical Writer/User Assistance Developer at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure. I specialize in developing content for an internal engineering audience. I use my writing skills and eye for detail to produce content that has the quality of customer-facing documentation. I develop content with the help of brilliant subject matter experts, Confluence, HTML, Acrolinx, Git, and Bitbucket. My goal in writing is to create usable, accessible, refreshing, and elegant documentation.
